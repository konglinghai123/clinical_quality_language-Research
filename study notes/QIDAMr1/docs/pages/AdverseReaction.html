Class AdverseReaction

  • Parent class: Observable
  • An adverse event caused by exposure to some agent (e.g., a medication, immunization, food, or environmental agent). An adverse reaction can range from a mild reaction, such as a harmless rash to a severe and life-threatening condition. It can occur immediately or develop over time. For example, a patient may develop a rash after taking a particular medication.
