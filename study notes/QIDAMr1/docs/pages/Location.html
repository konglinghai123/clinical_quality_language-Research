Class Location

  • Parent class: Entity
  • Details about a physical place where services are provided and resources and participants may be stored, found, contained or accommodated. A location includes both incidental locations (a place used for healthcare without prior designation or authorization) and dedicated, formally appointed locations. Locations may be private, public, mobile or fixed and scale from small freezers to full hospital buildings or parking garages. Examples of locations are:
    • Building, ward, corridor or room
    • Freezer, incubator
    • Vehicle or lift
    • Home, shed, or garage
    • Road, parking place, or park
