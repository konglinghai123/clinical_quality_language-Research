Class ManifestedSymptom

  • Signs and symptoms that were observed as part of the event.

    • Field Summary

      Fields 
      Field Type and Description
      bodySite BodySite
      Body site of the symptom or sign.
      criticality Code
      Characterizes impact on life or durable impact on physiological function or quality of life. Includes concepts such as life-threatening or potential loss of function or capacity, e.g., life threatening, potentially requires hospitalization, self-resolving. Different from severity in that a moderate subarachnoid hemorrhage is likely to be highly important, whereas a moderate headache is not.
      effectiveTime TimePeriod
      Time from when the symptom started to when they subsided.
      severity Code
      Characterizes the intensity of the manifestation of the sign or symptom. Includes concepts such as mild, moderate, severe. If the symptom is rash and severity is moderate, it means that the symptom was a moderate rash.
      symptomCode Code
      Specific sign or symptom that was observed.
