Class StatementOfUnknownOccurrence

  • Parent class: ClinicalStatement
  • A record of something of clinical relevance generally made by a patient, practitioner, or system stating that it is not known that the statement's topic + modality has occurred. For example, a patient may assert that they do not know if they had rheumatic fever in their childhood. This is an explicit assertion about an unknown occurrence. This is not the same as there being NO STATEMENTS about the patient having had rheumatic fever in their childhood (which could be due to missing or incomplete records).
